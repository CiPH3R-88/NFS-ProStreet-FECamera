NFS ProStreet FE Camera Mod by VeeTec & CiPH3R

Take full control over the Frontend, Car Lot, and Garage cameras in Need for Speed: ProStreet. 
This mod provides an in-game UI to tweak your camera's FOV, Orbit, 
and coordinates in real-time, making it incredibly easy to set up the perfect angles. 

Features & Controls
* Toggle Menu: Press F1 to open or close the overlay.
* Custom Saves: Hit the "Save File" button to export your exact slider settings. 
The mod will automatically generate a .nfsms script (named after the active VLT angle) 
inside the scripts/NFSPS_FECamera folder.
* VltEd & OGVI Ready: The exported .nfsms files are fully formatted and 
ready to be imported permanently into your game using VltEd or OGVI.

How to Install
1. Open the NFSPS_FECamera_Mod.rar archive.
2. Drag and drop the extracted files directly into your main NFS ProStreet game directory.
